How-to Guide for {{ university}} Students

Step 1

Log into Mosaic and Navigate to Student Center

Step 2

Click on the Enroll link

Step 3

Select the semester. Only Fall 2015 is support right now*

Step 4

Click Weekly Schedule view

Step 5

Click the little calender icon and select a date that show your whole week calendar. For example Sept 14th, then click Refresh Calendar

Step 6

Right-click the Save-As

Step 7

Save the webpage as "Webpage,Complete"

Step 8

Go to the location the file was save in, and navigate to the Student Center_files folder

Step 9

Now you will find your course Calendar HTML-file under a name similar to SA_LEARNER_SERVICES.SSR_SSENRL_SCHD_W.html

Add Calendar to Google Calendar

 Step 1 - Open Google Calendar – Be sure you are logged into a Gmail Account
Step 2 - Just to the right of "Other calendars" on the left sidebar of your Google Calendar you will see a downwards facing arrow. Click on this arrow and then select "Import calendar"
Step 3 - Find your recently saved calendar file and select or create a new Calendar to drop all your dates into

Add Calendar to MacBook/IPhone/Android

 For Mac-Book : Just Double click and open the .ics file, import to iCal
For IPhone : Send .ics file to IPhone, and open the .ics file, import to iCal
For Android: Download Google Calendar from Google Play, send .ics file to Android, and open the .ics file with Google Calendar
